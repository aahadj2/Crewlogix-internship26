SECTION: LUMS OVERVIEW
================================================================================
Lahore University of Management Sciences (LUMS) was founded in 1984 and is
located in DHA, Lahore, Pakistan. LUMS is a leading research university with
triple-crown accreditation (AACSB, AMBA, EQUIS).

Schools at LUMS:
  1. Suleman Dawood School of Business (SDSB)
  2. Mushtaq Ahmad Gurmani School of Humanities and Social Sciences (MGSHSS)
  3. Syed Babar Ali School of Science and Engineering (SBASSE)
  4. Shaikh Ahmad Hassan School of Law (SAHSOL)
  5. Rausing Executive Development Centre (REDC)

Contact:

  Email  : info@lums.edu.pk
  Website: www.lums.edu.pk


SECTION: UNDERGRADUATE ADMISSIONS
================================================================================
Requirements for BSc / BA / LLB programs:
- Higher Secondary School Certificate (HSSC / A-Levels / IB) with at least 60%
- SAT score (1200+ recommended) or NTS-NAT score
- LUMS Admission Test (LAT) required for most programs
- Personal statement and letters of recommendation
- Interview may be required for shortlisted candidates

Application Deadlines (approximate):
- Early Decision  : October 31
- Regular Decision: January 31

Transfer Students:
- Up to 50% of credits may be transferred
- Minimum 2 semesters must be completed at LUMS


SECTION: GRADUATE ADMISSIONS
================================================================================
MBA Program (SDSB):
- Bachelor's degree with 3+ years of professional work experience
- GMAT 550+ or equivalent GRE score
- Rolling admissions; 2-year full-time program, 60 credit hours total

MS Programs (SBASSE / MGSHSS):
- Bachelor's degree in a relevant field
- GRE General Test required
- Research proposal required for thesis track
- 30-36 credit hours

PhD Programs:
- Master's degree preferred; direct BSc entry considered case-by-case
- GRE and detailed research proposal mandatory
- 60+ credit hours beyond Bachelor's degree


SECTION: ACADEMIC POLICIES — CREDIT HOURS AND LOAD
================================================================================
Credit Hour Rules:
- Full-time minimum : 12 credit hours per semester
- Part-time minimum :  9 credit hours per semester
- Maximum          : 21 credit hours (Dean's written permission required)
- Standard load    : 15-18 credit hours per semester
- 1 lecture hour/week = 1 credit hour; labs = 1 credit hour per 2 lab hours

GPA Scale (4.0 system):
  A  = 4.0    A- = 3.7
  B+ = 3.3    B  = 3.0    B- = 2.7
  C+ = 2.3    C  = 2.0    C- = 1.7
  D+ = 1.3    D  = 1.0    F  = 0.0

Academic Standing:
- Good Standing      : CGPA >= 2.0
- Academic Probation : CGPA < 2.0 for two consecutive semesters
- Academic Dismissal : CGPA < 1.5, or probation for three consecutive semesters
- Dean's List        : Semester GPA >= 3.7 with at least 15 registered credit hours


SECTION: ATTENDANCE POLICY
================================================================================
- Minimum required attendance: 80% of all scheduled class sessions
- Missing more than 20% leads to grade deduction or WF (Withdrawal Failing)
- Three consecutive unexcused absences are reported to the Office of Student Affairs
- Medical leave: a certificate from the LUMS Health Centre must be submitted within 3 days
- Students on Academic Probation must maintain 100% attendance unless formally excused

Course Withdrawal Options:
- Drop (no record on transcript) : within the first 2 weeks of semester
- Withdrawal (W grade, no GPA impact): up to week 10 of semester
- Withdrawal Failing (WF, counts as F): after the week-10 deadline
- Medical Withdrawal: permitted with full documentation; no academic penalty


SECTION: EXAMINATION AND ASSESSMENT POLICIES
================================================================================
Typical Assessment Breakdown:
  Quizzes / Assignments : 20%
  Mid-term Exam         : 30%
  Final Exam            : 40%
  Participation/Project : 10%

Examination Rules:
- Students must carry a valid LUMS student ID to all exams
- Calculators permitted only if explicitly stated on the exam paper
- No electronic devices unless approved in writing by the instructor
- Late arrival beyond 30 minutes: not permitted to sit the exam
- Re-grading requests must be submitted within 5 working days of result release

Academic Integrity:
- Plagiarism results in automatic F grade and Academic Integrity Committee referral
- Turnitin is used for all written submissions
- Exam cheating results in suspension (minimum 1 semester) or permanent expulsion
  for repeat offenses

SECTION: SCHOLARSHIP PROGRAMS
================================================================================
Need-Based Scholarships:
- LUMS National Outreach Programme (NOP):
  Full scholarship — tuition + stipend + accommodation — for students from
  underprivileged or remote backgrounds.
- LUMS One Nation Programme:
  Similar to NOP; targets students from underdeveloped regions of Pakistan.
- General Need-Based Aid:
  25% to 100% tuition waiver based on financial need assessment.

Merit-Based Scholarships:
- Chancellor's Award        : 100% tuition waiver for top-ranked incoming students
- Dean's Merit Scholarship  : 50-75% tuition waiver for exceptional academic performance
- Academic Excellence Award : 25-50% tuition waiver for consistently high CGPA

Scholarship Renewal Conditions:
- Need-based scholarships : maintain CGPA >= 2.5 each semester
- Merit-based scholarships: maintain CGPA >= 3.0 each semester
- Failure to meet CGPA threshold triggers a scholarship review or reduction

External Scholarships Available:
- HEC Need-Based Scholarship (Government of Pakistan)
- Fulbright Scholarship (for graduate studies abroad)
- Corporate scholarships: Atlas Honda, Engro, Lucky Cement, and others


SECTION: STUDENT CODE OF CONDUCT
================================================================================
General Conduct:
- Respectful behavior toward faculty, staff, and all students is mandatory
- Dress code: smart casual; overly revealing clothing not permitted on campus
- Smoking is prohibited inside all buildings; designated outdoor areas only
- Alcohol or drugs on campus: immediate suspension pending investigation

Academic Misconduct includes:
  Plagiarism, cheating, data fabrication, unauthorized collaboration

Disciplinary Process:
  Step 1 — Warning         : first minor offense
  Step 2 — Probation       : repeated or moderately serious offense
  Step 3 — Suspension      : 1 semester to 2 years
  Step 4 — Expulsion       : severe or repeat violations

Student Rights:
- Right to appeal any disciplinary decision within 14 days of notification
- Right to submit a written or in-person statement before any major penalty
- All disciplinary records are kept strictly confidential

SECTION: HOSTEL AND ACCOMMODATION POLICIES
================================================================================
Eligibility:
- Priority given to students whose permanent residence is outside Lahore (>50 km)
- Fresh (first-year) admissions receive priority allocation
- Returning students re-apply each year; allocation based on distance and financial need

Hostel Rules:
- Curfew: 11 PM for first-year students; 12 AM for second year and above
- Visitors of opposite gender permitted in common areas only, never in rooms
- Noise restrictions enforced after 10 PM; violation leads to warning then eviction
- Alcohol or drugs result in immediate eviction and disciplinary referral
- Cooking in rooms is not permitted; a common kitchen is available on each floor

Hostel Facilities:
- High-speed Wi-Fi, laundry machines, common TV room, prayer area, 24/7 security
