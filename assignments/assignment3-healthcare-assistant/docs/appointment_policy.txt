APPOINTMENT BOOKING POLICY - Dr. Jawad Zaheer Clinic

How to Book an Appointment:
1. Call the clinic reception directly at 042-35701771, or the booking line at 042-38900939.
2. Alternatively, patients can book online through the clinic's oladoc.com listing by selecting the desired doctor and an available time slot.
3. Walk-in patients are also accepted, but online or phone booking is strongly recommended to reduce waiting time.
4. When booking, please provide your full name, contact number, and the reason for the visit (e.g. general check-up, follow-up, lab test consultation).
5. A confirmation SMS or call will be sent once your appointment slot is confirmed.

Arrival Time:
Patients are requested to arrive at least 15 minutes before their scheduled appointment time to complete registration and vitals check (blood pressure, weight, temperature).

Rescheduling and Cancellation:
- Appointments can be rescheduled or cancelled by calling the clinic reception at least 3 hours before the scheduled time.
- Repeated no-shows without cancellation may result in a requirement to pay a booking confirmation fee for future appointments.

Late Arrival:
Patients arriving more than 20 minutes late may be asked to wait for the next available slot or rebook, depending on the doctor's schedule that day.

Documents to Bring:
- Any previous prescription or medical reports related to the current visit
- A valid CNIC or identification card for registration
