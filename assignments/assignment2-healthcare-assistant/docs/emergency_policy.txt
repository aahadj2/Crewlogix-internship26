EMERGENCY GUIDANCE - Dr. Jawad Zaheer Clinic

Important: This clinic is an outpatient general physician clinic and does NOT operate a 24/7 emergency room or trauma center.

What To Do In Case of a Medical Emergency:
1. For a life-threatening emergency (e.g. severe chest pain, difficulty breathing, unconsciousness, severe bleeding, suspected stroke), immediately call Pakistan's national emergency rescue service at 1122, or go directly to the nearest hospital emergency room.
2. Do NOT wait for a clinic appointment in a genuine emergency situation.
3. If you are unsure whether your situation is an emergency, call the clinic reception at 042-35701771 for guidance, but prioritize calling 1122 or visiting the nearest hospital ER if symptoms are severe.
4. Nearby hospitals with 24/7 emergency departments should be used for urgent, life-threatening situations; ask clinic reception for the nearest recommended hospital if needed.

After-Hours Guidance:
- Outside of clinic hours (before 9:00 AM or after 11:00 PM), the clinic reception phone line may not be answered. In this situation, please go directly to the nearest hospital emergency room or call 1122.

The clinic staff and this assistant are not able to provide medical diagnosis, treatment advice, or medication recommendations for emergency or urgent symptoms. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional or emergency service directly for such situations.
