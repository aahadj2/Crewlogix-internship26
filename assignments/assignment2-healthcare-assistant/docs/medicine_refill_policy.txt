MEDICINE REFILL POLICY - Dr. Jawad Zaheer Clinic

Eligibility for Refill Without a New Visit:
- Patients with a stable, chronic condition (e.g. hypertension, diabetes, thyroid) who have visited the clinic within the last 3 months may request a medicine refill prescription without an in-person visit.
- Refill requests are subject to the treating doctor's review and approval.

How to Request a Refill:
1. Call the clinic reception at 042-35701771 and provide your name, CNIC, and the medicine/prescription details.
2. Alternatively, request a video consultation with Dr. Fatima Jawad through oladoc.com for a refill review.
3. The clinic will confirm within 24 hours whether the refill has been approved and is ready for collection, or whether an in-person visit is required.

When an In-Person Visit is Required:
- If more than 3 months have passed since the last consultation.
- If the patient reports any new symptoms, side effects, or changes in condition.
- If the medication is a controlled substance requiring an in-person prescription by law.

Refill Collection:
- Approved refill prescriptions can be collected from the clinic reception. There is no separate fee for a straightforward refill approval, though a follow-up consultation fee may apply if the doctor requests a check-up.

Please note: The clinic cannot approve refills for medications originally prescribed by a different doctor or hospital without a review consultation.
