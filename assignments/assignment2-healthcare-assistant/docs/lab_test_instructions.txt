LAB TEST INSTRUCTIONS - Dr. Jawad Zaheer Clinic

Fasting Blood Sugar (FBS) Test:
- Patients must fast for 8 to 10 hours before the test (an overnight fast, e.g. after dinner until the morning sample, is recommended).
- Plain water is allowed and encouraged during the fasting period to stay hydrated; however, tea, coffee, juice, milk, and any sugary or flavored drinks are NOT allowed.
- Patients should take their routine medication only after the sample has been collected, unless the doctor has advised otherwise.
- Smoking and chewing gum should also be avoided during the fasting window as they may affect results.

Lipid Profile (Cholesterol) Test:
- Requires 10 to 12 hours of fasting. Only plain water is permitted during this period.

HbA1c (Average Blood Sugar) Test:
- No fasting is required for this test. It can be done at any time of the day.

Complete Blood Count (CBC) and Routine Tests:
- No special fasting is required unless combined with a fasting test on the same visit.

General Instructions for All Lab Tests:
- Bring your doctor's prescription or test request slip to the lab.
- Inform the lab staff of any current medications you are taking, as some may need to be paused before certain tests.
- Sample collection is available at the clinic reception between 8:00 AM and 10:00 AM for fasting tests.
- Test reports are typically available within 24 hours and can be collected in person or via the clinic's online report portal.
