PATIENT REPORT COLLECTION POLICY - Dr. Jawad Zaheer Clinic

Collecting Lab Reports:
- Most lab test reports are ready within 24 hours of sample collection.
- Reports can be collected in person from the clinic reception between 9:00 AM and 11:00 PM, any day of the week.
- Please bring your appointment slip or CNIC when collecting a report in person.

Collecting Reports on Behalf of Someone Else:
- A family member may collect a report on the patient's behalf by presenting the patient's CNIC copy or the original test receipt/slip.

Online / Digital Reports:
- Where available, reports can also be sent via WhatsApp or email if requested at the time of sample collection. Please provide your correct contact details to the lab staff.

Report Retention:
- The clinic retains a digital copy of patient reports and prescriptions for internal record-keeping. Patients may request a duplicate copy of a past report by calling the reception with their name and approximate visit date.

Discussing Reports with the Doctor:
- If you would like the doctor to explain or review your report, please book a follow-up consultation rather than requesting an explanation over the phone, so the doctor can review your full medical history properly.
